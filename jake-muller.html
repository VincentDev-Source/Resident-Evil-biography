Jake Muller

Mercenary, Key to a Cure
Archive ID • JM-06 • Restricted Bloodline
First Major IncidentGlobal C-virus crisis in Resident Evil 6
Known ForPossessing antibodies tied to Albert Wesker’s bloodline
Lore RoleA living contrast between inheritance and personal choice

Biography

Jake Muller enters the series during Resident Evil 6 as a tough mercenary who initially wants little to do with anyone else’s war. His importance becomes obvious when his blood shows unique properties that can help counter the C-virus.

As the son of Albert Wesker, Jake carries one of the most dangerous legacies in the series. The tension in his story comes from whether bloodline determines destiny. Instead of becoming another tyrannical figure, Jake gradually chooses to protect others.

His partnership with Sherry Birkin gives his story emotional weight, tying two characters shaped by past experiments into a narrative about survival, distrust, and the possibility of breaking from history.

Relationships

Sherry Birkin: partner and emotional anchor Jake and Sherry begin with tension and suspicion, but they grow into one of Resident Evil 6’s strongest partnerships. Many fans read their bond as romantic or potentially romantic.
Albert Wesker: father by blood, not by loyalty Jake is Wesker’s son, but he rejects the legacy of domination and cruelty tied to his father’s name.
Chris Redfield: uneasy respect Chris sees Jake through the lens of Wesker’s history, which creates tension before the larger crisis forces them into reluctant cooperation.
