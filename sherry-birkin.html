Sherry Birkin

Survivor, Government Agent
Archive ID • SB-02 • G-Virus Survivor
First Major IncidentRaccoon City outbreak in Resident Evil 2
Known ForSurviving childhood trauma and later fighting bio-terror herself
Lore RoleA child victim who grows into a major operative

Biography

Sherry Birkin first appears as a child caught in the middle of the Raccoon City disaster and her parents’ catastrophic G-virus research. Her early story shows the cost of Umbrella-linked science on the innocent.

By Resident Evil 6, Sherry has matured into a government agent, carrying both the physical consequences of past experimentation and the emotional resilience that lets her keep moving forward.

Sherry’s arc is one of the clearest examples of a Resident Evil character refusing to be defined by trauma, even when the series repeatedly drags old horrors into the present.

Relationships

Claire Redfield: protector and mentor figure Claire protects Sherry during Resident Evil 2, creating a bond built on safety, compassion, and survival.
Jake Muller: partner with romantic tension Sherry and Jake share one of Resident Evil 6’s most emotionally grounded partnerships, built on trust formed under pursuit, experimentation, and global crisis.
William and Annette Birkin: tragic family legacy Sherry’s parents define the tragedy at the center of her childhood, leaving her to live with the cost of their choices long after they are gone.
