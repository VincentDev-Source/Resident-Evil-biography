Field Profile

Leon S. Kennedy

Police Officer, Government Agent
First Major IncidentRaccoon City outbreak in Resident Evil 2
Known ForSurviving impossible missions with precision and resolve
Lore RoleOne of the series’ clearest witnesses to Umbrella’s human cost

Biography

Leon arrives in Raccoon City on his first day as a police officer and is immediately thrown into a citywide nightmare. That beginning defines his arc: a rookie forced to grow under pressure while confronting the truth behind Umbrella’s crimes.

After surviving the city’s destruction, Leon is recruited into U.S. government service. He becomes a specialist in anti-biohazard operations, eventually handling high-risk missions involving national security and global bio-terror threats.

In Resident Evil 4, Leon’s rescue mission for Ashley Graham places him against Los Illuminados and Las Plagas, proving that the biohazard era has moved beyond viruses alone. His story balances action-hero skill with the lingering trauma of what he witnessed in Raccoon City.

Relationships

Ada Wong: romantic tension Their connection is one of the most famous in the series. Leon cares about Ada, but her secrecy and shifting loyalties keep the relationship unresolved and dangerous.
Claire Redfield: trusted ally Leon and Claire survive Raccoon City together. Their bond is built on mutual respect rather than romance.
Ashley Graham: protector and partner in crisis Leon acts as Ashley’s protector during Resident Evil 4, guiding her through trauma while staying focused on the mission.
